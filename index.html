About the Author

Veena Sinha is a celebrated author and poet whose voice spans the breadth of human experience — from the intimate corners of domestic life to the vast landscapes of memory and longing. Writing with equal fluency in English and Hindi, her work has earned acclaim across literary communities in India and beyond.

With eighteen published titles spanning literary fiction, novels, and poetry collections, Veena brings a rare depth of feeling to every page. Her prose is known for its lyrical precision, and her poetry for the way it holds silence as meaningfully as words.
